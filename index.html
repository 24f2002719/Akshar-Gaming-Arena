Frequently Asked Questions
What gaming systems are available?
We offer high-end gaming PCs, PlayStation 5 consoles, PlayStation 4 Pro consoles, Racing Simulators, VR2 headsets, Pool tables, Snooker tables, and Foosball tables. All our systems are regularly maintained and updated with the latest games and hardware.
How many games are available to play?
We have an extensive library of over 100+ games across all platforms. This includes latest PS5 titles, popular PC games, VR experiences, and classic favorites. Our collection is regularly updated with new releases.
Do you host gaming tournaments?
Yes! We organize weekly tournaments for popular games like CS2, Valorant, FIFA, and more. Prize pools and tournament schedules are announced on our social media channels and in-store displays.
What are your operating hours?
We're open daily from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Extended hours are available during tournaments and special events. Holiday hours may vary.
Do you offer membership plans?
Yes, we offer various membership plans: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, and Premium memberships. Each plan comes with different benefits including discounted rates, priority booking, and exclusive tournament entry.
What amenities are available?
We provide comfortable gaming chairs, high-speed internet, air conditioning, snack bar, refreshments, and a relaxation lounge. Free Wi-Fi is available for all visitors.
